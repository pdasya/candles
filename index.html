The stages of creating candles

Creating a candle from soy wax can consist of the following stages:

  1. Materials Preparation: Small pieces of soy wax, candle wick, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials.

  2. Preparing the Mold: Selecting the candle mold and preparing it for wax filling.

  3. Preparing the Wax: Melting soy wax in a melting device to the required temperature. Adding fragrances and dyes if necessary.

  4. Wick Preparation: Inserting the wick into the mold and securing it in a way that it is evenly tensioned.

  5. Materials Preparation: Small fragments of soy wax, candle wick, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials.

  6. Filling the Mold: Pouring the wax into the mold until it is completely filled. Waiting for it to solidify.
